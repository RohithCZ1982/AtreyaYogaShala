Classical Practice

Hatha Yoga

All Levels · Ages 16 and above

Hatha Yoga is the root — the foundation from which all modern yoga flows. In Sanskrit, Ha means sun and Tha means moon, representing the balance of opposing forces within us. These classes work systematically through the classical asanas, pranayama (breath regulation), and guided meditation, weaving together the physical and the subtle.

Suitable for complete beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Each session is structured to build heat, open the body, steady the mind, and close with deep rest. Expect honest teaching, intelligent sequencing, and Sanskrit nomenclature to honour the tradition.

  • Classical asana practice with alignment guidance
  • Pranayama — Nadi Shodhana, Kapalabhati, Bhramari and more
  • Meditation and dharana (concentration) techniques
  • Yoga Nidra and guided Savasana
  • 60 – 75 minute sessions, live and interactive
  • Morning & evening batches available

For Young Ones

Kids Yoga

Ages 5 – 14 · Playful & Nurturing

Children possess a natural openness — they learn through play, story, and imagination. Kids Yoga harnesses that quality, introducing yoga and mindfulness in ways that feel like adventures, not lessons. Each class weaves postures into stories, animals into asanas, and breathing into games.

Beyond flexibility and coordination, children develop focus, emotional regulation, and the simple but profound ability to pause and breathe. Seeds planted now become forests of wellbeing. Classes are conducted in small groups to ensure every child feels seen.

  • Story-based and animal-themed asana sequences
  • Simple breathing exercises for calm and focus
  • Mindfulness games and relaxation techniques
  • Builds concentration — wonderful for school performance
  • 45-minute sessions, live and interactive
  • Separate batches for ages 5–9 and 10–14

For Expectant Mothers

Prenatal Yoga

All Trimesters · With Doctor's Clearance

Pregnancy is one of the most profound journeys a human body undertakes. Prenatal Yoga honours this by offering gentle, safe, and deeply nourishing practice that supports mother and baby at every stage. Nothing is rushed, nothing is forced — this is yoga as care.

Carefully crafted sequences ease the discomforts of pregnancy — back pain, swollen ankles, breathlessness, sleeplessness — while building the strength and endurance needed for labour and delivery. Breathwork prepares the nervous system. Meditation prepares the heart. Connection deepens between mother and the growing life within.

  • Safe asanas adapted for all three trimesters
  • Pelvic floor strengthening and hip-opening sequences
  • Breathing techniques for labour — Ujjayi, slow exhales
  • Guided relaxation and bonding meditation
  • Postnatal recovery guidance on request
  • 60-minute sessions with personal modifications

Please consult your doctor before joining. All health considerations are taken seriously.
